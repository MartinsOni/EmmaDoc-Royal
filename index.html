About EmmaDoc Royal Germany GmbH

EmmaDoc Royal Germany GmbH is your reliable, leading independent importer and wholesaler of petroleum products in Europe. We are also active in the trading of all finished petroleum products.

As the demand from service stations and major clients cannot be entirely satisfied locally through their own refinery production, the logistics are further optimised through the reciprocal supply and joint storage of products from the oil companies. This generates substantial reductions in terms of transport traffic, storage space and the associated costs, noise and emissions.
